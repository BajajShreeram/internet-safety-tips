Welcome to Internet Safety Tips

Here are some important tips for staying safe online:

  • Never share your password with others.
  • Do not respond to strangers who message you online.
  • Avoid downloading files from unknown websites.
  • Report any bullying or harmful content you see online.
  • Always tell an adult before meeting someone you've only met online.
  • Be careful when clicking on links from unknown sources.
  • Do not share personal information like your home address or school name.
  • Use strong, unique passwords for all your accounts.
  • Log out of accounts when using shared or public computers.
  • Only use trusted websites for shopping and avoid suspicious ads or pop-ups.
  • Keep your software and antivirus up-to-date to protect against malware.
  • Talk to an adult about your online activities and ask for help if something makes you uncomfortable.
